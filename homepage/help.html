Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

How do I submit a review?

Click on the “Submit Review” link in the navigation or footer, then fill in your matatu details and rating.

What if I can't find the matatu I want to review?

You can still submit a review by entering the name or plate number manually — we’ll add it to the system.

Are the ratings verified?

All ratings are submitted by real commuters. We monitor content for spam or abuse, but they are community-based.

How can I contact the MatBuzz team?

Use the contact page or email us directly at matbuzzsupport@gmail.com.

What happens to my data?

Your data is kept private and only used to improve the platform. You can read our full Privacy Policy here.

Still need help?

Reach out to us via the Contact Page.
